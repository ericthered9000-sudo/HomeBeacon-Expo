Request Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your HomeBeacon account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. In-App: Open HomeBeacon → Settings → tap "Delete Account"
  2. Email: Send a deletion request to support@homebeacon.app with subject "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the request
  4. We'll process your request within 30 days
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent. You'll lose access to:
  • Your wellness check-in history
  • Medication reminders and tracking
  • Family connections and invite codes
  • All account settings and preferences

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • ✅ Account credentials (email, password hash)
  • ✅ Profile information
  • ✅ Wellness check-in history
  • ✅ Medication tracking data
  • ✅ Emergency contact information
  • ✅ Family/friend connections
  • ✅ Invite codes you've created
  • ✅ Push notification tokens

What Data May Be Retained

  • 📊 Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (no personal identifiers)
  • 📋 Crash logs retained for 90 days for debugging
